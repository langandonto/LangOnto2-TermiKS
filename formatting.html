Joint Second Workshop on Language and Ontology & Terminology and Knowledge Structures (LangOnto2 + TermiKS)

Submission Requirements

Long papers should be a maximum of 8 pages long with tow additional pages allowed for references. Demo and poster papers should be a maximum of 4 long with two a additional pages allowed for references. Articles should be anonymised and each article will be reviewed by 3 reviewers. The accepted articles will be part of the conference proceedings and will be published in an electronic form in the ACL Anthology.

Papers should be electronically submited in a PDF format at the following link:

https://www.softconf.com/lrec2016/LO2TKS2016/

The deadline for submission is 21st February 2016.

Formatting

Please use the LREC 2016 template.
